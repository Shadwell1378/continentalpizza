Menu Our Mission

A World on Your Plate.

Nine unique pies, nine unforgettable journeys.

North America

The Diner Supreme

The ultimate classic—cheesy, meaty, and bold.

Toppings:

Pepperoni, Italian Sausage, Sautéed Mushrooms, Green Peppers, and Onions.

South America (Argentina)

The Argentine Fugazza

An Italian-Argentine hybrid that focuses on sweet, savory onions.

Toppings:

**No Sauce** (White Pie), Mozzarella, a generous layer of **Caramelized Sweet Onions**, Oregano, and a drizzle of premium Olive Oil.

Europe (Italy)

The Roman Artichoke

A true Mediterranean masterpiece with cured ham and tangy vegetables.

Toppings:

Tomato Sauce, Fresh Mozzarella, **Prosciutto di Parma** (added after baking), Marinated Artichoke Hearts, and Fresh Basil.

East Asia (Japan/Korea Inspired)

The Umami Eel

Sweet, savory, and rich, blending traditional ingredients with a pizza base.

Toppings:

Soy-ginger glaze base, Mozzarella, Cooked **Eel (Unagi)** or Teriyaki Chicken, Sweet Corn, a swirl of **Kewpie Mayonnaise**, and Nori (seaweed) flakes.

West Asia (Middle East)

The Lebanese Za'atar Pie

Earthy, tangy, and aromatic, a classic flatbread flavor profile.

Toppings:

Oil base, Akawi/Feta Cheese blend, Ground Lamb seasoned with **Sumac** and Allspice, finished with fresh **Za'atar** and a touch of Harissa.

North Africa (Morocco)

The Moroccan Tagine

Warm, complex spices balanced with fruit and heat.

Toppings:

Light Tomato Base, Mozzarella, Lamb/Chicken seasoned with **Ras el Hanout**, Sweet **Dried Apricots**, Cilantro, and a swirl of **Harissa Paste**.

South Africa

The Braai Wors

Celebrating the national barbecue culture with local favorites.

Toppings:

Tomato Base, Mozzarella, sliced **Boerewors** (coarse sausage), Caramelized Onions, Creamy Feta, and fresh **Avocado** (added after baking).

Australia & Oceania

The Aussie BBQ

A classic down-under combo—sweet, smoky, and salty.

Toppings:

**BBQ Sauce Base**, Mozzarella, Shredded Chicken, Bacon, Red Onion, and sweet Pineapple chunks.

Antarctica (Dessert Pizza)

The Polar Ice Cap

A refreshing finish, inspired by the chill of the South Pole.

Toppings:

**Cream Cheese 'Sauce'** Base, Crushed **Peppermint Candies**, White Chocolate Chips, finished with a dusting of **Powdered Sugar** (snow) and a dark chocolate drizzle.
